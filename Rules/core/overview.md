# Overview

Online board game themed around fishing ships, fish trading, and light territorial pressure.

## Objective

Players win by accumulating the most coins before the match ends.

## Game fantasy

- sail through seas with different risk levels;
- catch valuable resources;
- sell at good timing;
- survive sea hazards;
- contest space with other players indirectly or directly, depending on mode.

## Design direction

- matches short enough for project evaluation;
- clear rules for clients and server;
- visible risk/reward in every choice;
- low waiting time between turns.

## Additional idea

To improve readability, each sea can have a strong visual identity: calmer, more profitable, more dangerous, or more unstable. This helps players understand strategy without reading too many rules.
