# Quick Mode

Mode designed for evaluation and shorter matches.

## Goals

- reduce match duration;
- simplify cards and actions;
- keep environmental risk as the main pressure source;
- avoid direct player-vs-player combat.

## Main rules

- 4 to 6 rounds;
- no hard ship limit per sea;
- hazard level increases when more players share the same sea;
- simpler cards with immediate effects;
- focus on movement, fishing, and selling.

## Balancing direction

- crowded seas should become more dangerous;
- empty seas should be safer but less profitable;
- cards should provide small advantages, not long combo chains.

## Additional idea

Quick mode can be the version used in presentation and project evaluation. This makes the experience easier to demonstrate and lowers the risk of matches taking too long.
