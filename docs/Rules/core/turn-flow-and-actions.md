# Turn Flow and Actions

Each round can be divided into simple phases.

## Suggested order

1. Movement
2. Sea hazard
3. Mandatory fishing
4. Market
5. Pending effect resolution

## Core actions

- move to another sea;
- repair ship in the lagoon by paying gold;
- repair hull;
- reinforce ship;
- play a card;
- sell fish in the lagoon;
- hold fish for a future set.

## Movement rule

- Each player may move once per round.
- A player may move to any sea if it is free.
- If the target sea is occupied, a battle starts.
- The loser of the battle always returns to the lagoon.
- The winner occupies the target sea.

## Repair rule

- Repairing the ship is only possible in the lagoon.
- Repair costs gold and restores HP.
- The exact repair price can be fixed or scale with missing HP.

## Additional idea

For quick mode, keep one main decision per turn plus one movement attempt. This reduces downtime and avoids long turns. In normal mode, this layer can expand with more interactions and reaction cards.
