# Fishing and Economy

## Fish types

- Common: Sardine, Anchovy
- Uncommon: Tuna, Mackerel
- Rare: Swordfish, Blue Salmon
- Epic: Golden Sunfish

## General logic

- Fish can only be sold in the lagoon, which acts as the shared market hub.

## Suggested sets

- Local School: 3 different common fish.
- Export Set: 2 uncommon + 1 rare.
- Ocean Trophy: 2 rare + 1 epic.

## Additional idea

The economy becomes more interesting when players face two conflicting choices: sell quickly to lock in coins, or hold cargo to complete a better set later. This works well in both quick and normal modes.
