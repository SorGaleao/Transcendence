# Cards and Hazards

## Basic cards

- Quick Movement: move with no extra cost.
- Light Repair: recover health.
- Naval Shield: reduce damage this turn.
- Improved Net: increase fishing output.
- Favorable Sale: improve sale value.

## Sea hazards

- Storm
- Hidden rocks
- Strong current
- Sea monster

## Environmental damage table (V1)

Roll 1d6 for the sea where the player ends the turn.

- Lagoon:
	- Any result: 0 damage.
- Outer A:
	- 1-2: 0 damage
	- 3-6: 1 damage
- Outer B:
	- 1: 0 damage
	- 2-5: 1 damage
	- 6: 2 damage
- Outer C:
	- 1: 0 damage
	- 2-3: 1 damage
	- 4-6: 2 damage
- Outer D:
	- 1: 0 damage
	- 2-4: 2 damage
	- 5-6: 3 damage

## Persistence pressure

- If a player stays in the same outer sea for a consecutive turn, add +1 environmental damage (cap +1).
- The modifier resets when changing sea or returning to the lagoon.

## Additional rule

In quick mode, hazard level can scale with sea crowding. In normal mode, this same system can coexist with more complex events, such as attack cards, blocks, or interference between players.
