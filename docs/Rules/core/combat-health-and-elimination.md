# Combat, Health, and Elimination

## Base state

- Suggested starting HP: 10

## Damage sources

- sea hazards;
- offensive-effect cards;
- direct combat in normal mode;
- crowding pressure in quick mode.

## When HP reaches zero

- the ship sinks;
- the player loses all fish cargo;
- the player loses a percentage of gold (suggestion: 25% to 40%, rounded down);
- they return to the lagoon with partial HP on the next turn or the following turn, depending on the mode.

## Additional rule

To avoid early exclusion, respawn can happen with a controlled penalty, but the default rule should be: full cargo loss, partial gold loss, and return to the lagoon.

## Repair dock

- The lagoon contains the repair dock.
- Players may spend gold there to restore HP.
- Suggested repair rule: pay a fee per missing HP, or pay a flat fee to restore the ship to full HP.
- Repairing should only be possible in the lagoon.
