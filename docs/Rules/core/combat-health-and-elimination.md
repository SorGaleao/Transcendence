# Combat, Health, and Elimination

## Base state

- Suggested starting HP: 10

## Damage sources

- sea hazards;
- offensive-effect cards;
- direct combat in normal mode;
- crowding pressure in quick mode.

## When HP reaches zero

- the ship sinks;
- the player loses part of their cargo;
- they return with partial HP on the next turn or the following turn, depending on the mode.

## Additional rule

To avoid early exclusion, respawn can happen with a controlled penalty: fish loss, coin loss, or a less favorable position. This preserves participation and reduces frustration in short matches.
