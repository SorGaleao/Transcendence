# Map and Seas

The board has 5 seas:

- 1 central sea
- 4 outer seas around the center

## Difficulty progression

From safest route to riskiest:

1. Outer Sea A
2. Outer Sea B
3. Outer Sea C
4. Outer Sea D

## Role of each sea

- Central Sea: support, recovery, and low risk.
- Outer A: safest entry point for better fishing.
- Outer B: mid-level area with balanced outcomes.
- Outer C: high risk with better rewards.
- Outer D: most dangerous sea with highest rewards.

## Additional rule

Instead of being only for movement, each sea should work as a decision zone: the more crowded and dangerous it is, the more valuable it becomes. This makes route choices meaningful even without direct combat.
